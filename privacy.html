Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Your Privacy Matters

We care about your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

What We Collect

  • Basic Details:
    • Name
    • Phone number
    • Birthday
  • Content You Share:
    • Photos and videos you post
    • Comments and reactions
    • Your group memberships

How We Use Your Info

  • To help you set up and manage your account
  • To show your content to people in your groups
  • To keep you updated about Crü’s features and changes
  • To understand how to make Crü better for everyone

What We Don’t Do

  • We don’t sell your personal information.
  • We don’t share your data with advertisers for marketing.

Keeping Your Data Safe

We use industry-standard security measures to keep your data safe. Your information is encrypted and protected against unauthorized access.

Your Choices

We believe you should be in control of your data. You can:

  • Delete your account whenever you want
  • Download a copy of your data
  • Update your personal details
  • Decide who sees your posts by adjusting group settings

Need Help?

Have questions or concerns? Please reach out. We’re here to help you understand and manage your privacy.
